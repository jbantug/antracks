== Description ==
This is a youtube playlist plugin.

== Installation ==
1.) Download the zip file.
2.) Extract zip file to plugins folder on wordpress.
3.) On you dashboard, click on plugins and Activate Antracks plugin.
4.) Go to your wordpress folder->wp-content->plugins->newantracks.
5.) Open newantracks.php
6.) On line 26 of newantracks.php, edit the localhost, username, and password to your corresponding database localhost name, username, and password
7.) To add playlists on your database, go to your wordpress dashboard and click on Settings.
8.) Under the Settings, click on antracks. There you can record playlist on your database.
9.) On the antracks admin page, fill in your desired playlist name and copy paste your chosen playlist link. Then click submit.
10.) After submitting your playlist entry, choose the playlist on the dropdown menu and choose the autoplay option. After that, click submit and a text area will appear showing the shortcode.
11.) The shortcode looks like this: [antracks id='playlist name']. Copy and paste this shortcode to your page/post and save.
12.) View the page to which your playlist was embedded.
